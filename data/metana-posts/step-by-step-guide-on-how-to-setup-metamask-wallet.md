### Why Use MetaMask?


MetaMask allows interactions between the Ethereum blockchain and a crypto wallet. While it defaults to the Ethereum network, other EVM-compatible chains can be added in a few simple steps. Without further ado let‚Äôs jump into **how to setup Metamask** wallet 


### Installing the Wallet


Download it on either Chrome, IOS, or Android at <https://metamask.io/> and install it.  




![how to setup Metamask](https://metana.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image.png)
### How to setup metamask: The Steps


1. Click ‚ÄúGet Started‚Äù to begin setting up a wallet.



![Metamask welcome screen](https://metana.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-2.png)
2. ‚ÄúClick the Create a Wallet‚Äù button if you‚Äôre setting up a new wallet; otherwise, click ‚Äúimport wallet‚Äù if you already have a seed phrase/ account.



![new to metamask?](https://metana.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-3.png)
3. Another prompt will show up asking if you want to help improve it. Accepting or rejecting will have no impact on your wallet.



![Help us improve Metamask](https://metana.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-4.png)
4. Choose a wallet password to keep your wallet safe, this is not your seed phrase. Click on ‚ÄúCreate‚Äù to continue.



![create password for metamask wallet](https://metana.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-5.png)
5. Watch the video to learn about the Secret Recovery Phrase



![video on seed phrase by metamask](https://metana.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-6.png)
6. Click to reveal the seed phrase, write it down and keep it in a secure place and do not share it with anyone, then click ‚Äúnext‚Äù.



![secret recovery phrase ](https://metana.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-7.png)
7. Repeat the recovery phrase to confirm you jotted it down correctly.



![confirming seedphrase](https://metana.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-8.png)
8. Click ‚ÄúAll done‚Äù and your wallet is now set up!



![congratulations page](https://metana.io/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/image-9.png)
